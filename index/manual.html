1. Enter the Required Values:
    • Force (N) – Input the force applied in newtons.
    • Time (s) – Enter the time during which the force is applied in seconds.
    • Mass (kg) – Provide the object's mass in kilograms.
    • Initial Velocity (m/s) – Type in the object's velocity before the force was applied.
    • Final Velocity (m/s) – Type in the object's velocity after the force was applied.
  2. Click the "Calculate" Button:
    • The calculator will immediately compute and display:
      • Initial Momentum
      • Final Momentum
      • Change in Momentum
      • Impulse
  3. Optional Buttons:
    • View Last Calculation – View the previous results.
    • Clear Results – Reset all fields to enter new values.
  4. Check the Calculation Logs:
    • Your computed values will appear in the Calculation Logs section on the right.

⚠️ Make sure to fill in all fields with valid numbers. Invalid or empty fields will show a warning message.
