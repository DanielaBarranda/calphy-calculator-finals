Impulse is the change in momentum of an object when a force is applied over a period of time. It tells us how much the motion of an object has changed.
    
Formula: J = F * T

    Where: 
      J = Impulse (N * S or KG.m/s)
      F = force applied (N)
      T = time force is applied (S)
      Δp = change in momentum
      Δv = change in velocity
